Corporate vs Business Differentiation

Corporate directives govern the overarching architectural boundary of our multi-business enterprise. The key focus here centers completely on portfolio resource allocation, parenting advantages, and structural market mapping.

Aggressive Reinvestment
High market growth, dominant absolute positioning.
Selective Extension
High growth capability with weak structural scale.
Harvest / Yield Capture
Mature business units generating liquid returns.
Divestment Allocation
Stagnant segments failing to hurdle WACC metrics.

Conversely, Business Unit actions prioritize building defensible competitive positions inside isolated spaces using differentiation or cost leadership variables.
